<objective>
Create the new internal dimension-scoring service that replaces the deleted ZEN/MoSCoW engine with equal-weight per-dimension averaging (D-02, D-03, SCOR-01/02), and the server-side completion gate (D-06/D-07, SCOR-04) that all five score/report endpoints will call before scoring. This plan is purely additive — no existing file is modified, so the app remains importable and the full suite stays green.

Purpose: Establish the single source of scoring truth (`compute_dimension_scores`) and the single completion gate (`assert_assessment_complete`) that Plans 02 and 03 wire into the existing endpoints, and that Plan 04's removal work depends on being in place first.
Output: `backend/app/services/dimension_scoring.py` (new service) plus `backend/tests/services/test_dimension_scoring.py` (unit coverage for SCOR-01/02 and the gate).
</objective>
